Assignment 2

IT Work, the Interview.

Demographic

Senior Data Engineer from the financial sector of their company.

1. What kind of work is done by the IT professional?

The IT professional that our group interviewed is one of our group member’s mum. The IT
professional is a Senior Data Engineer in the financial sector of the company she works at.
The kind of work done by this IT professional includes mostly writing sQL scripts. She mostly
helps develop and write programs for the data sets she works with and provides a lot of
support to those she works with. This work is considered more of the backend work in IT
rather than the frontend work as she spends little time working with customers directly.

2. What kinds of people does the IT professional interact with? Are they other IT
professionals? Clients? Investors? The general public?

The interactions this IT professional has is primarily within the company. She works closely
with analysts, architects and IT operation support.

3. Where does the IT professional spend most of their time?

Most of our IT professional’s time is spent in the office at a desk behind a computer. The only
times that she really leaves her desk will be for team meetings and other essential needs. As
she is working on the backend of her work, she does not spend time consulting with clients or shareholders.

4. What aspect of their position is most challenging?

The most challenging part in their position at their company is production support. Often
there will be issues that are ones that you would not see often and working around those
take more time. With issues, they are important to the work that she does as it can
help her in future when she comes across the same or similar issue again. These issues are
used to learn to adapt and improve future work.

Extra info:

During university, she did a Computer Science degree and that’s where her love of
programming began. We then asked our IT professional about what she enjoys about her
job, her response to it was mostly around programming. She enjoys programming a lot and
started her search for jobs as a programmer initially. Eventually she moved towards
supportive roles and that is what got her to her position today as a Senior Data Engineer.
